Understanding the Calculation

This simulator provides a unique perspective on scarcity by comparing the max supply of Kaspa to that of Bitcoin. This is not a comparison of price or market cap, but a projection of your relative share within the total ecosystem.

  • Bitcoin Max Supply: 21,000,000 BTC
  • Kaspa Max Supply: 28,700,000,000 KAS

The calculation determines what share of Bitcoin's total supply you would own if your percentage ownership of Kaspa's total supply were transposed.

BTC Equivalent = (Your KAS / KAS Max Supply) * BTC Max Supply

The Kaspa Revolution

The BlockDAG: Beyond Blockchain

Unlike Bitcoin, which processes blocks linearly, Kaspa uses a Block Directed Acyclic Graph (blockDAG) structure. This allows it to process multiple blocks in parallel, dramatically increasing network efficiency and speed.

100% Fair Launch

Kaspa was launched in November 2021 with no pre-mining, no pre-sales, and no coin allocations to the team or investors. It is a fully decentralized, open-source, community-managed project in the true spirit of Bitcoin.

Extreme Speed & Scalability

Kaspa can handle very high block rates (currently 10 per second, aiming for 100). Transactions are confirmed in seconds, making it ideal for everyday payments and solving the blockchain trilemma without compromise.
